Pick Assist

What this tool does & how it works
• Paste data: VL06F (“requirements”) on the left and LX02 (“stock”) on the right. The app parses the SAP pipe tables (|…|).
• Allocation logic: For each VL06F line (in order), the tool allocates full Storage Units (pallets) from LX02 by FEFO (earliest SLED/BBD first). It never breaks a pallet and never over‑allocates. If the next FEFO pallet is larger than the remaining need, it keeps scanning for a smaller pallet that fits.
• When exact coverage isn’t possible, the tool stops and writes a SHORT summary for the remainder.
• Output layout: Rows are physically grouped by Delivery → Material. The first OK row per material in a delivery shows Dlv.Qty and Sales Unit and is styled bold (also in Excel). SHORT rows always display both.

VL06F (Requirements)

Must include: Pick Date, Loadg Date (or Loading Date), Pur. Doc., Delivery, Material, Item Description, Name of Sold-to Party, Name ship-to party, DlvTy, Dlv.Qty, Route, Carrier Name.

LX02 (Stock)

Must include: Material, Batch, Stor. Bin, Avail.stck, BUn, Storage Unit, SLED/BBD.
